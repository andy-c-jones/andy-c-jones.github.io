Geek Retreat is a great shop and event space on Union Street, right next to Glasgow Central railway station.
<br>
<br>
Comics and games are this shops speciality but what makes this a more unique venture is its cafe. The main part of the shop is an area of tables and chairs where events are held almost every night. Whether you like Pokemon, Yu-Gi-Oh, Magic: The Gathering or D&D style board games there is a weekly event for you. Most of the events are free to participate it but it is expected of you to utilise the cafe if you are there for any length of time. The cafe serves basic food stuffs from toasties to hotdog as well as hot and cold drinks. My only complaint would be that it doesn’t serve many healthy options and does, in my opinion, promote the idea of the unhealthy geek in a basement (the shop is in a basement). That being said, if you are into any of the games that are played there or just enjoy comic books then this is the place to go in Glasgow to meet new people and make friends.
<br>
<br>
It is open late in the evenings and at the weekend so check it out after work or check their events page for your game of choice.
