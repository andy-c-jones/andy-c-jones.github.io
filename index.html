ThoughtWorks

I love my job. As a consultant for ThoughtWorks I travel all over the UK delivering software projects and coaching developers of all levels. Primarily, coaching focuses on agile methodologies and new technologies.
One of the greatest things about working as a consultant is the large set of environments that I work with. I get exposure to many different platforms, languages, programming paradigms and tools.
